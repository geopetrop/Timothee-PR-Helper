Timothée is also scheduled for visible co-branded activity tonight with **Maison Valeur**, a fictional luxury fragrance partner.

## Timing and approvals

- Timothée is off-phone in prep until **6:05 PM**.

- The manager can approve **sponsor and reporter holding replies** before then.

- That holding line must work before full facts are available and must be **40 words or fewer**.

## Talent profile

- Timothée is a high-visibility actor with a large online audience and a polished public image.
- He is not generally known for chaotic posting.
- Because the clip is arts-related, the backlash is spreading across celebrity, culture, and institution accounts. That increases meme risk and makes the story harder to contain.

## Voice guide for outward-facing language

Aim for:
- calm, self-aware, and human
- specific without sounding over-lawyered
- respectful to the affected audience
- accountable for impact without overstating unverified facts

Do not:
- make **"you misunderstood me"** the whole message
- argue with commenters
- try to joke this away
- sound condescending about ballet, opera, or "old" art
- center awards-night excitement while backlash is active
